## Backup Timing Rules

- If the app is already open and responsive, go straight to the saved demo run.
- If the app is slow, skip the optional fresh-run explanation.
- If the laptop struggles, switch to `pitch/assets/full-run-page.png` and keep moving.
- Never spend more than `30 to 45 seconds` troubleshooting live.

## Success Conditions

- Judges understand why this is relevant to Southlake specifically.
- Judges see the product working, even if only through the saved run.
- Judges leave with a clear memory of the caution boundary.
- The team finishes early rather than running out of time.
